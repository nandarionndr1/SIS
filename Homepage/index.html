History

WMC was established in February 2, 2002. It was created by virtue of a board resolution by the following members: Dr. Manuel R. Guerrero, Ms. Anna Karenina Guerrero-Corpuz, Mr. Albert A. Corpuz, Mr. Rosano A. Corpuz, and Mr. Karlo Rodovilio C. Guerrero.

Philosophy

Westside Montessori Centrum believes that all children have the innate ability to learn if given the opportunity to receive a quality education. Every child is unique and with guidance and support can use their special gift to make a difference in themselves and our world.

Objectives

  • Being responsive to change as presented by ASEAN Integration
  • Engaging with critical and contemporary issues national and international
  • Prioritizing humanity and culture to uplift the quality of life

Mission

To prepare students to understand, contribute to and succeed in a rapidly changing society.

Vision

To be the preeminent intellectual and creative center for effective engagement in a world that increasingly demands better-designed objects, communication, systems and organizations to meet social needs.
